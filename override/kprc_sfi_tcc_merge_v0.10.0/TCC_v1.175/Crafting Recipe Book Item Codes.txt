The books are adde to the vendors. If you can't visit the vendors for whatever reason, you can add the books using the console commands:

Open up the console.

DebugMode 1
giveitem n2_crft_book_recipe_tcccreate
giveitem n2_crft_book_recipe_tccenchant
giveitem n2_crft_book_recipe_tccenchant_a
giveitem n2_crft_book_recipe_tccenchant_w
DebugMode 0

Close the console.
